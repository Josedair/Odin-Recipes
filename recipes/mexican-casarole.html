Mexican Casarole

  • Prep: 20 mins
  • Cook: 30 mins
  • Total: 50 mins
  • Servings: 6

"This 'Mexican' casserole is easy to make and very tasty. So good it will have you saying 'ay carumba'. I often substitute with ground turkey and low-fat dairy products, and it's still delicious! Serve this casserole with chips, salsa, and green salad.

Recipe by ANDREALF63

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of lean ground beef
  • 2 cups salsa
  • 1 (16 ounce) can chili beans, drained
  • 3 cups tortilla chips, crushed
  • 2 cups sour cream
  • 1 (2 ounce) can sliced black olives, drained
  • ½ cup chopped green onion
  • ½ cup chopped fresh tomato
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Spray a 9x13-baking dish with cooking spray.
  2. Cook ground beef in a large skillet over medium-high heat until no longer pink, 8 to 10 minutes.
  3. Stir in salsa, reduce heat, and simmer until liquid is absorbed, about 20 minutes.
  4. Stir in beans; cook until heated through.
  5. Spread crushed tortilla chips in the baking dish, then spoon beef mixture on top. Spread sour cream over beef, then sprinkle olives, green onion, and tomato on top. Cover with Cheddar cheese.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until hot and bubbly, about 30 minutes.
  7. Enjoy :)
